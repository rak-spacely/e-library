Quick Answers

Is Placid Projects really free?

Yes. All books in our core library are completely free to download. We offer an optional premium membership that provides early access to new releases and additional features, but the majority of our content is freely accessible to everyone.

Can I request specific books?

Absolutely. While we cannot guarantee every request can be fulfilled due to licensing requirements, we track all requests and do our best to add highly requested titles. Use the contact form above to share your request.

What formats do you offer?

Currently, all books are available as PDF downloads, optimized for both desktop and mobile reading. We are working on adding EPUB and MOBI formats in the near future.

How can I support Placid Projects?

The best way to support us is to use the platform and share it with others who could benefit. We also accept donations and offer a premium membership. If you are an author or educator, consider contributing content to our collection.
