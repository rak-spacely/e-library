Our Story

About Placid Projects

Learn how a shared passion for knowledge became a global digital library.

Placid Projects Limited was founded in 2018 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality educational content accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or economic circumstances. Our founders, a diverse group of educators, librarians, and technology entrepreneurs, recognized that while information has never been more abundant, access to structured, curated learning materials remained a challenge for millions worldwide.

What started as a collection of 500 public domain books has grown into a comprehensive digital library housing over 15,000 titles across dozens of subjects. We have partnered with independent authors, educational institutions, and publishers who share our commitment to open access education. Every book in our collection has been carefully selected, properly formatted, and optimized for digital reading.

Today, Placid Projects serves over 2 million learners from 120 countries. Students use our platform to supplement their coursework, professionals access it for career development, and lifelong learners explore new interests and hobbies. We have seen our books downloaded in rural villages with limited internet, in bustling urban libraries, and everywhere in between.

We believe that education is the foundation of personal growth and societal progress. In a world where textbooks can cost hundreds of dollars and library access is not universal, we are working to level the playing field. Every download represents someone taking a step toward their goals, and that motivates us every day.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

Our mission is to eliminate barriers to education by providing free, high-quality digital books and learning resources to anyone with an internet connection. We are building a global library without walls, where curiosity is the only requirement for access.

Vision

We envision a world where knowledge flows freely, where a student in any country can access the same educational materials as their peers in the most privileged institutions, and where the pursuit of learning is limited only by imagination, not circumstances.

Core Values

Accessibility First

We design our platform to be simple, fast, and usable on any device. Whether you are accessing us from a smartphone on a slow connection or a high-speed desktop, your experience matters. All our books are properly formatted, searchable, and compatible with screen readers.

Quality Over Quantity

While we are proud of our extensive collection, we do not add books just to inflate numbers. Every title is reviewed for accuracy, readability, and educational value. We curate, not just aggregate.

Community Driven

Our users help shape our collection through download analytics, ratings, and requests. We regularly survey our community to understand what subjects and formats serve them best. Many of our best resources come from user suggestions.

Transparency and Ethics

We clearly indicate licensing information for every book, respect copyright laws, and work only with authors and publishers who have granted appropriate permissions. We do not sell user data, and we are transparent about how our platform is funded, through donations and optional premium features.

Our Impact in Numbers

Over 2 million learners have downloaded books from our platform since 2018. Our most downloaded book, Introduction to Computer Science, has been accessed over 50,000 times, helping countless individuals begin careers in technology. Students in 120 countries have used our materials to supplement their education, with particularly strong adoption in India, Nigeria, Brazil, Indonesia, and the Philippines.

We have partnered with 45 universities and more than 200 independent authors who have chosen to share their work through our platform. Seventy eight percent of our users report that our resources directly contributed to achieving an educational or career goal. Perhaps most meaningfully, 35 percent of our users live in areas where physical libraries are not easily accessible.
